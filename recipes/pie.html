Pie Recipe

Back to Home

Description:

A pie is a baked dish which is usually made of a pastry dough casing that contains a filling of various sweet or savory ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1/2 cup ice water
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., fruit, custard, meat)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add ice water until the dough comes together.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and place it in a pie dish.
  5. Add your desired filling.
  6. Cover with a top crust if desired, and seal the edges.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
